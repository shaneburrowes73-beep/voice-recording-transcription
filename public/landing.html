The Problem

Generic transcription fails Caribbean professionals

Off-the-shelf tools were trained on American and British English — not how we speak in Barbados.

🎙️

High error rates on Caribbean accents

Standard STT engines misread Barbadian intonation, dialect vocabulary and Creole expressions, producing transcripts that need heavy manual correction.

⛓️

No chain of custody

Law enforcement and legal teams need every transcript to be reviewed, approved and locked by a supervisor. Consumer tools offer none of this.

🔓

Sensitive data in shared platforms

Police incident notes and legal records cannot share infrastructure with other organisations. Off-the-shelf tools cannot guarantee this isolation.
